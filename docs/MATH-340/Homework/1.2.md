4. Solve the system of equations

    $$
    \begin{align}
    x &= 3 - 4z + 4y \\
    4x + 5y &= 3z
    \end{align}
    $$

    Using $t$ as a parameter, the solution is:

    $$
    \begin{bmatrix}
    x \\
    y \\
    z
    \end{bmatrix}
    =
    \begin{bmatrix}
    -\frac{8}{21}t + \frac{5}{7} \\
    \frac{19}{21}t - \frac{4}{7} \\
    t
    \end{bmatrix}
    $$
